Privacy Policy — SYCOPHANCY.md

What we collect

We collect only standard web server logs through Cloudflare: your IP address, user agent, timestamp, and page visited. This is automatic, standard CDN practice for infrastructure and security.

Cloudflare's built-in analytics provide usage statistics only — no personal data tracking, no cookies, no analytics scripts beyond Cloudflare.

What we don't collect

We do not:

  • Collect personal data (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Track you with cookies or fingerprinting
  • Run analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Track email opens or link clicks
  • Have forms that submit data to our servers
  • Run third-party scripts for advertising or tracking

Third parties

Cloudflare — our CDN and hosting provider. Read Cloudflare's privacy policy for details on what they collect.

Google Fonts — font delivery service. Font requests may log IP addresses. Read Google's privacy policy for details.

No advertising or external analytics platforms.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email info@sycophancy.md.
